Peppermint Patties

Serves: 2

### Ingredients ###
	A
		450ml whole milk
		75ml single cream
	
	B
		70g 70% dark chocolate
		30g milk chocolate
		Pinch of salt

	C
		50ml peppermint schnapps
		50ml creme de cacao
		20ml creme de menthe


### Method ###
	1)	[A] + [B] in saucepan
	2) 	Heat until [B] melts into [A]
	3)	Continue heating until at desired temperature
	4)	Add [C]
