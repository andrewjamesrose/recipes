Fondue

Serves: 4

### Ingredients ###
	2 garlic cloves
	Olive oil
	400g emmental (grated)
	400g gruyere (grated)
	350ml Sauvignon blanc
	1 shot of kirsch
	2 heaped teaspoons of cornflour
	Grated nutmeg (?)
	
Method
	0)	Dissolve the cornflour in the 
	1)	Soften the garlic in oil
	2)	Add the wine and heat
	3)	Add cheese in paired handfuls, simultaneously add the kirsch+cornflour mix
	4)	Whisky continuously to maximise phase mixing
